Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the begining of the game.

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal below you will add a specific amount to the points of your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose when your total score goes over the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the crystal will have a new random number.
